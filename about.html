About Know Patience Fragance

Know Patience Fragance is a quiet stand against vulgar institutional luxury.

Elegant fine fragrances are crafted in small batches—each one painstakingly designed to evoke confident presence, not noise.

We believe smell is intimate and personal. Scents are the bridge connecting memories with emotions.

And elegance is never abrasive.

Based in the United States, we work with clients who value timelessness over trends, and individuality over imitation.
